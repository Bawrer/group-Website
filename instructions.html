Zombie Shooting Game

How to Play:

Welcome to our Zombie Apocalypse Game! Follow these instructions to get started:

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move your character.

Kill as many zombies as you can, to earn points.

The game advances when your gun changes. You'll see the zombies coming towards you in more numbers, and faster.

Use the mouse to aim and click to shoot.

Controls:

The following controls are available:

Arrow keys or WASD keys: Move

Mouse: Aim

Left mouse button: Shoot
